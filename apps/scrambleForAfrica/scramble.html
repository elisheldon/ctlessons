The Scramble for Africa

Before we jump into the game, you need to know how it works! To the left you can see Madagascar broken into a grid. In the full game, each team will take turns claiming squares of Africa. Which squares you can claim are based on what year it is, what country you are and where your other squares are. Once a square is claimed, it belongs to that country forever. Squares that you can claim will glow.

Move your cursor over some of the squares. Any square with land will show you its climate, its resources and which country it belongs to. To continue, claim the two glowing squares to the left.
You can change how map squares are colored and which information is displayed by using the options beneath the map. To the right of the map, you'll see the year and whose turn it is up at the top. You have a limited amount of time each turn, so make sure you're ready with a decision!

Below that, you'll see your objectives (which will change over time!) and what you can do on your turn, followed by recent activity and the current scores. Highest score at the end of Year 1895 is the winner!

Once you're ready to begin, click the button below - and good luck!
